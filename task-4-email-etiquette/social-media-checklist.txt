Social Media Do's and Don'ts

Do's:
1. Maintain a professional and respectful tone
2. Verify information before sharing
3. Use strong privacy settings
4. Share useful and positive content
5. Be mindful of your digital footprint

Don'ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information
2. Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
3. Do not engage in cyberbullying
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links
5. Do not spread misinformation
