Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone.
2. Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve unexpected payment requests.
3. Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
6. Avoid clicking on suspicious links in messages or emails.
7. Regularly check your bank statements for unauthorized transactions.
8. Install apps only from trusted sources like official app stores.
9. Never send money to receive money (common UPI scam trick).
10. Immediately report fraud to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call helpline 1930.
